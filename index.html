Please enter your name exactly as you would like it to appear on a public list of the 2014 Capital Pro Bono Honor Roll. Please list the firm where you were employed at the time you performed the 50+ hours of pro bono work, even if you no longer are employed at that firm.

Many law firm pro bono administrators submit applications on behalf of all the firm's attorneys eligible for the Honor Roll. If your law firm has a pro bono administrator, please ask whether an application has been submitted on your behalf to avoid duplicate submissions.

Please review your information prior to submitting your application. No final review screen will appear once you submit your application.
